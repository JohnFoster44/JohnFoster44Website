Local Hair Salon & LGBTQIA+ Community Project

The Riah Hair Studio
HTML, CSS, JavaScript & (Interact.js, Amplitude.js, jQuery UI)

The Riah Hair Studio is a new salon that stands strong on promoting inclusion in the beauty industry by ditching current beauty standards and allowing the freedom to have a comfortable, judgement-free salon experience.

The website was developed alongside the founder to present the salon in all its amazing uniqueness. Including a music player, draggable desktop style windows and a Snake Mini-game.
