Jake Winsor

Available for session work.

Musical theatre and pop sessions with access to home recording equipment.

Jake is studying Music Industry Practice at the Academy of Contemporary Music - specialising in guitar. He has studied under Giorgio Serci, (Jools Holland, Shirley Bassey, BBC concert Orchestra, Yamaha) Toby Drummond (Wicked The Musical, Shaun Ryder, Cliff Richard) and Manolo Polidario (Fame!, Colin Blunstone).

Whilst at ACM, Jake spends his spare time gigging and sitting in on West End musicals, absorbing as much information as possible to prepare him for the road ahead.

Jake has played with a number of outfits across the UK; well established singers, songwriters, covers bands and as part of a pit band ensemble. Co-founding and running a corporate events band* gave him the drive to pursue his degree.

*(playing venues such as, Twickenham Stadium and Hampton Court Palace)

Spending his teen years heavily gigging on weekends* further opportunities arose from a local theatre company where Jake found work for multiple pantomime seasons and musical theatre workshops. He practiced his sight reading, playing to a click and got to performin under Musical Direction from David Redstone (Rambo, First Blood).

*(corporate events, functions, pubs, clubs, Isle of Wight Festival and Bestival)

Having his first gig at 14 - 11 years later Jake is active in the events and entertainment industry playing lead guitar, rhythm guitar, as part of an ensemble and as performing as a soloist playing acoustic guitar and singing.
