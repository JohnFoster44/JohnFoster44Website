The website for Forest of Four - my brothers band.

It was my first try at making a website from scratch so I kept it nice and simple. Following the vibe of the album cover I went with dark blue/ black colour palette. I was quite happy with it being my first design with a purpose. In hindsight I think that for usability I could have kept it to one page.

Adobe XD design of this website. Seeing as it's free I thought why not give it a go! I really like this, it helped me focus on a design I was going for and have an end goal. There are some differences on the Xd design and this website - things I would like to implement when I know more (albums opening on the music page) and looks that I ended up preferring (this page).
