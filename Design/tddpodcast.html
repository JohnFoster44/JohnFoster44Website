The Dating Debacle - Podcast

I was asked by two podcasters to do a cover for their upcoming dating podcast.

They gave me a rough idea of what they had in mind, it had to be themed around romance and online dating. Their rough idea was too cluttered, but I liked the plastered heart for branding. I was thinking ahead to when they'd want a clean and recognisable logo. It needed to be simple and stand out.

I rolled with the plaster on the heart and came back with the design above - a bright, clear and simple logo that could be recognised on small screens easily.
