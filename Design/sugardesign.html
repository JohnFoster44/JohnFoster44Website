Sugar Glaze - Twitch Streamer

I made this comission by modifying a bubbly looking font to make the text have a more organic shape to fit the doughnut theme of the channel.

Making the banner design fit the twitch layout was the trickiest task, at the time twitch had two panels on the left and right of the screen that the user could expand and shrink.

Some of the drafts would get cut off/ be off centre when the panels were triggered. I managed to find a size that stayed centered no matter which panel was open. Twitch has since changed their layout, no longer making that an issue.

I think the design pops and suits the channels vibe a lot, Sugar Glaze was happy with the result too!
